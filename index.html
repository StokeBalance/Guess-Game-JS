Instructions on how to play the game

The game is of four (4) stages.

In stage 1, you have only one chance to guess the right number between 1 and 2. If your guess is right, you move to the next stage, otherwise you lose the game. The range of the guess continues increase by one up to the final stage, and your chances increase to three (3) in all the other stages. If your guess is right within the given trial up to the last stage, then you are crowned champion!
